INNOVASCI OPEN UNIVERSITY - REFACTORING COMPLETE
================================================

This comprehensive refactoring transforms the system from a Polytechnic platform 
(focused on ND/HND diplomas) to an Open University system supporting BSc, PGD, MSc, 
and PhD degrees with strict portal separation.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
==================

1. TYPE SYSTEM (src/types/index.ts)
   - ManagementPortalRole union (16 roles)
   - AcademicPortalRole union (11 roles)  
   - ProgrammeType: 'bsc' | 'pgd' | 'msc' | 'phd'
   - PortalType: 'management' | 'academic' | 'student'
   - ROLE_TO_PORTAL mapping
   - School & SchoolType entities
   - Removed: EntryCategory ('ND' | 'HND')

2. RBAC SYSTEM (src/lib/rbac.ts)
   - New ROLE_PERMISSIONS matrix (29 roles, 600+ permissions)
   - Helper functions: getPortalForRole(), canAccessPortal()
   - Management vs Academic vs Student permission separation
   - Portal-specific permission grouping

3. PORTAL SEPARATION (src/lib/portal-separation.ts - NEW)
   - validatePortalAccess()
   - getAuthorizedPortal()
   - isManagementPortalUser()
   - isAcademicPortalUser()
   - isStudentPortalUser()
   - PORTAL_RESTRICTIONS configuration
   - Login redirect routing

4. DEMO CREDENTIALS (src/lib/seed-data.ts - NEW)
   - 35 demo users across all roles
   - Management (16): Vice-Chancellor, Deputy VCs, Directors
   - Academic (11): Deans, HOD, Coordinators, Tutors
   - Student (6): Various degree levels and types
